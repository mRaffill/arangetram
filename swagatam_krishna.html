Swagatam Krishna

Ragam: Mohana, Talam: Adi, Composer: Oottukkaadu Venkata Subbaiar, Choreographer: Guru Vidhya Subramanian

Pallavi:
Refrain:
Swaagatam krishha saranaagatam krishnaa
Welcome, Lord Krishna, and grant me refuge
Madhuraapuri sadanaa mridu vadanaa madhusoodana iha
Dweller of Mathura, with a charming face, destroyer of the demon Madhu
Anupallavi:
Bridge:
Boga Thaptha sulabha supushpa gandha kalapa
You are open to those who truly seek you, you are adorned with fragrant flowers,
Kasthuri thilaka mahitha mama kantha nanda gopa kandha
You wear the Kasturi mark on your forehead, you are honored by all, you are the baby son of Nanda Gopa
Charanam:
Lyrics:
Mushtikasura Chanoora malla, Madhu soodhana, Kuvalayapeeda,
Vanquisher of the wrestlers Mushtika and Chanoora, the demon Madhu and the elephant Kuvalayapita
Mardhana, Kalinga narthana, Gokula Rakshana, Sakala sulakshana,
You danced on Kaalinga, you saved Gokula, you spread blessings to all,
Deva sishta jana pala, Sankalpa kalpa, kalpa satha koti samprabhava,
You protect the devas and the ordinary folk, your will is the foundation of time, you rise across hundred crores of eons,
Dheera, Muni jana vihara, Madana sukumara, Daithya samhara, deva,
You are brave, have dwelt with sages, are the beautiful god of love, have killed Asuras,
Madhura madhura rathi sahasa, sahasa Vruja yuvathi jana manasa poojitha
The young maidens of Vraja worship you for your sweetness and boldness
