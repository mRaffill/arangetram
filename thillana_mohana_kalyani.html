Thillana Mohana Kalyani

Ragam: Mohana Kalyani, Talam: Adi, Composer: Lalgudi Jayaraman, Choreographer: Guru S. K. Rajaratnam Pillai

sundara mOhana rUpam
A beautiful and captivating form
vandita muni janAnandam
that grants bliss to the sages who worship you
santatam valli kAntam
Beloved of Valli, we always
cintayE guruvaram skandam
think of Lord Skanda, the best of teachers
