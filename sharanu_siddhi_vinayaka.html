Sharanu Siddhi Vinayaka

Pallavi:
Sharanu siddhi vinayaka
Sharanu vidya pradayaka

Anupallavi:
Sharanu parvati tanaya muruti
Sharanu mooshika vahana

Charanam:
nitila netrana varada putrane
nagabhusasa priyane
tatila tankita komalangane
karna kundala dharane
 
kuksi maha lambodarane
iksu capana gelidane
paksi vahana siri purandara
vittalana nija dasane

Refrain:
I take refuge in the meditation master Vinayaka
I take refuge in the one who imparts knowledge.

Bridge:
I take refuge in Parvati's son
I take refuge in the one whose steed is his mouse.

Lyrics:
Son of consort of God with eyes on forehead,
God who loves to wear serpent ornament,
Pretty God who is decorated with Gingelly,
God who wears studs in his ears.
 
God who has a big paunch and belly,
Who won over the God who holds a sugar cane bow,
The god with the bird steed - Purandara
Vittala's true devotee
