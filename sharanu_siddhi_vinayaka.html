Sharanu Siddhi Vinayaka

Ragam: Natai, Talam: Mishra Chaapu, Composer: Purandara Daasar, Choreographer: Guru Vidhya Subramanian

Pallavi:
Refrain:
Sharanu siddhi vinayaka
I take refuge in Vinayaka to achieve my goals
Sharanu vidya pradayaka
I take refuge in the one who imparts knowledge.
Anupallavi:
Bridge:
Sharanu parvati tanaya muruti
I take refuge in Parvati's son
Sharanu mooshika vahana
I take refuge in the one who rides a mouse steed.
Charanam:
Lyrics:
nitila netrana devi suthane
Son of the three eyed God Shiva and Goddess Parvati,
nagabhusasa priyane
adorned by a serpent tied around his belly,
takita tankita komalangane
whose beautiful limbs tap out the rhythm "takita tankita",
karna kundala dharane
who wears dangling golden earrings
kuksi maha lambodarane
who has a big paunch and belly,
iksu capana gelidane
who won over the god of desire,
paksi vahana sri purandara
The true devotee of
vittalana nija dasane
the bird-mounted Purandara Vittala (Vishnu)
