Gajananam Bhoota Ganathi Sevitham

Ragam: Natar, Talam: Adi

Gajaananam bhoota ganathi sevitam
The elephant-headed one who is served by the celestials,
Kapittha jambuu phalasaara bhakshitam
Who drinks the juice of the Kapittha and Jambuu fruits,
Umaasutam shoka vinaasha kaarannam
Who is the son of Uma and destroyer of sorrow,
Namaami vighneshwara paadapankajam
I salute the lotus feet of Lord Vighneshwara.

गजाननं भूतगणादि सेवितं
कपित्थजम्बूफलसार भक्षितम्
उमासुतं शोकविनाशकारणं
नमामि विघ्नेश्वर पादपङ्कजम्
